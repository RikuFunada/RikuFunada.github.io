Coordination of Robots over Long Distance

How to achieve coordinated control of robots in distant areas (e.g. different continents)?
Approach: Passivity-based Control and Control Barrier Function
This is a collaborative work between the Robotarium in Georgia Tech and the preliminary version of the Robot Zoo in Tokyo Tech.

  • Video1
  • Video2

Visual Coverage with Quadcopters

How to cooperatively monitor a wide important area by quadcopters equipped with cameras?
Approach: Coverage Control and Control Barrier Function

  • Video1
  • Video2

Visual Surveillance with PTZ Camera

How to cooperatively monitor dynamic targets, such as pedestrians, by cameras with controllable orientation?
Approach: Optimization on Manifolds and Density Estimation

  • Video

3-D Visual Human Localization

How to localize a pedestrian with cameras under the existence of obstacles which may cause occlusions?
Approach: Passivity based Distributed Optimization and Loop Shaping
